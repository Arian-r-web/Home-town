About Cumilla

Cumilla is a historically rich city in eastern Bangladesh. It is home to ancient Buddhist monasteries, serene landscapes, cultural landmarks, and significant war memorials. The city blends heritage with modern development, making it a unique destination for history lovers and travelers alike.
